1. Introduction

Grey Ink Projects ("the Agency", "we", "us", or "our") is a digital agency based in South Africa, providing web development, deployment, management, and Shopify services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at greyinkprojects.online (the "Site") or otherwise interact with us, such as through our contact form or WhatsApp.

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA") and, where applicable, other relevant data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company or brand name (optional)
  • Service of interest and project details submitted via our contact form
  • Any information you share with us via WhatsApp, email, or Instagram direct message

Information collected automatically

  • Standard technical data such as browser type, device type, and general usage patterns, to the extent provided by our hosting and font providers
  • A local browser storage flag indicating whether you have acknowledged our cookie notice (see Section 5)

We do not currently use third-party analytics or advertising trackers on this Site. If this changes in future, this Policy will be updated accordingly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide requested quotes or information
  • Communicate with you about a Project, including via email or WhatsApp
  • Deliver, maintain, and improve our services
  • Comply with our legal and contractual obligations
  • Maintain records of client engagements for legitimate business purposes

4. Third-Party Services

To operate this Site and process enquiries, we rely on a small number of trusted third-party service providers, including:

  • Google Fonts — used to load web fonts for the Site's typography.
  • EmailJS — used to deliver contact form submissions to our email inboxes.
  • InstantDB — used to securely store contact form submissions so we can follow up with you.
  • FormSubmit — used as a fallback delivery method for contact form submissions if our primary method is unavailable.
  • WhatsApp and Instagram — if you choose to contact us through these platforms, your interactions are also subject to their respective privacy policies.

These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their function and are not authorised to use your information for their own marketing purposes.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Our Site uses minimal, essential local browser storage — for example, to remember that you have dismissed our cookie notice. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information with the third-party service providers listed above, where required by law, or where necessary to protect our legal rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry, deliver services, and comply with our legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to Section 9 below.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights (POPIA)

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual retention requirements
  • Object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 13.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are intended for businesses and individuals over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (such as EmailJS, InstantDB, and Google Fonts) may process or store information on servers located outside South Africa. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure your information continues to receive an adequate level of protection.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
