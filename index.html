FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

Still have questions? Reach out and we will respond within 24 hours.

Ask Us Anything Chat on WhatsApp
How long does it take to build a website?
Most standard websites are delivered within 7 to 14 business days from when we have all your content and brand assets. Larger or more complex projects such as full e-commerce stores typically take 3 to 6 weeks.
What do I need to provide to get started?
To kick things off we typically need your brand assets (logo, colours, fonts if you have them), any written content for the site, and a clear brief. If you do not have content ready, we can help with that too.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. We offer monthly website management packages that cover content updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and ongoing support. You can choose to manage the site yourself or let us handle everything.
Can you work with my existing website?
Absolutely. We can redesign, rebuild, or improve your existing site. Whether it is a full overhaul or targeted improvements to specific pages, we assess what you have and work with you on the best path forward.
Do you build Shopify stores from scratch?
Yes. We build fully custom Shopify stores including themes, product setup, payment configuration, shipping rules, and app integrations. We also migrate existing stores from WooCommerce, Wix, or other platforms to Shopify.
How does billing work?
We work on a 50% upfront and 50% on delivery structure for project-based work. Monthly management packages are billed at the start of each month. All pricing is agreed upfront with no hidden costs.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Always. Every site we build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop. With the majority of web traffic coming from mobile devices, this is not optional for us; it is a standard part of every build.
