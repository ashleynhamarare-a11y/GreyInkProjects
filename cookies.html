1. What This Policy Covers

This Cookie Policy explains how Grey Ink Projects ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies, such as local browser storage, on greyinkprojects.online (the "Site"). It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website you visit. They are widely used to make websites function, work more efficiently, and provide reporting information. "Local storage" is a similar browser technology that allows a site to remember small pieces of information on your device between visits.

3. What We Use on This Site

Grey Ink Projects keeps things intentionally lean. Specifically, this Site uses:

  • Essential local storage — a single flag stored in your browser's local storage to remember that you have seen and dismissed our cookie notice, so we don't show it to you again unnecessarily.

We do not currently use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking cookies on this Site. Should this change in future — for example, if we add analytics to better understand how visitors use the Site — we will update this Policy and, where legally required, request your consent first.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages on this Site load resources from third-party providers, which may set their own cookies or collect data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — loads web font files. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS — used only when you submit our contact form, to deliver your enquiry by email.
  • WhatsApp and Instagram — if you click through to chat with us or view our profile, any cookies set are governed by Meta's respective privacy policies.

5. Managing Your Preferences

You can control or delete cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies and site data are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies and local storage when you close your browser

Please note that blocking essential local storage may cause our cookie notice to reappear on every visit, but it will not affect your ability to browse the Site or submit an enquiry.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the technologies we use or for legal and regulatory reasons. Please check back periodically for updates.
